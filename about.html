Our Story

PhysioPulse was founded with a simple mission: to help people in Hyderabad move better, feel better, and live better through expert physiotherapy care that actually works.

We saw too many people suffering from chronic pain, living with limitations, or recovering slowly from injuries because they weren't getting the right treatment. We knew there was a better way—one grounded in evidence, personalized to each individual, and focused on long-term results rather than quick fixes.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of patients across Hyderabad, helping them overcome pain, recover from injuries, and return to the activities they love. Our approach combines clinical expertise, modern techniques, and genuine care for every person who walks through our doors.

Meet Dr. Sankalp

Founder & Chief Physiotherapist

Dr. Sankalp is a highly qualified physiotherapist with extensive training and experience in advanced manual therapy techniques. He graduated from Nizam's Institute of Medical Sciences in Hyderabad with a Bachelor of Physiotherapy degree and has pursued specialized certifications including Osteopathic Techniques for Spine and Pelvis, Visceral Manipulation, Certified Dry Needling Practitioner from the United Kingdom, Fascial Manipulation Therapy from the Stecco Institute in Italy, and Advanced Training in Visceral Manipulation from the Barral Institute in Italy.

As a member of the International Association of Healthcare Practitioners (IAHP), Dr. Sankalp maintains the highest professional standards. With over 5,000 patients successfully treated, he brings exceptional expertise and a proven track record of excellent outcomes to PhysioPulse.

Dr. Sankalp founded PhysioPulse with a mission to provide cutting-edge advanced manual therapy that addresses the root cause of musculoskeletal problems with utmost care and passion. His comprehensive approach combines evidence-based techniques with personalized treatment plans to deliver lasting results for patients.

Our Philosophy

Evidence-Based Practice

We don't guess. Every treatment we provide is backed by clinical research and proven to deliver results. We stay current with the latest physiotherapy techniques and continuously update our methods based on new evidence.

Patient-Centered Care

Your goals are our goals. We listen carefully, understand your unique situation, and create treatment plans designed around your life, not a one-size-fits-all protocol.

Active Rehabilitation

We believe in empowering you with the knowledge and tools to take control of your recovery. You're not just a passive recipient of treatment—you're an active participant in your healing journey.

Long-Term Solutions

Our focus is on addressing the root cause of your problem, not just temporarily masking symptoms. We want you to stay healthy long after your treatment ends.

Our Approach

Every patient at PhysioPulse receives individualized care through a proven treatment process designed to deliver lasting results.

1

Comprehensive Assessment

We start by thoroughly understanding your condition, medical history, lifestyle, and goals. Our detailed assessment identifies the root cause of your problem, not just the symptoms.

2

Personalized Treatment Plan

Based on our findings, we create a customized treatment plan specific to your needs. We explain everything clearly so you understand what we're doing and why.

3

Active Treatment

We combine hands-on manual therapy, targeted exercises, and education to reduce pain, restore function, and build resilience. You're involved every step of the way.

4

Ongoing Support

We regularly reassess your progress and adjust your treatment as needed. We're with you until you achieve your goals—and we equip you to maintain your results independently.
