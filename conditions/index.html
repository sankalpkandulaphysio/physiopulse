Common Conditions We Treat

Evidence-based physiotherapy treatment for acute and chronic conditions affecting movement and quality of life.

Back Pain

Lower back pain, sciatica, herniated disc, spinal stenosis, and chronic back conditions affecting daily activities.

Neck Pain

Cervical strain, whiplash, tech neck, pinched nerves, and stiffness from poor posture or injury.

Shoulder Pain

Rotator cuff injuries, frozen shoulder, impingement syndrome, and shoulder instability affecting arm movement.

Knee Pain

ACL/MCL injuries, meniscus tears, runner's knee, arthritis, and post-surgical rehabilitation.

Ankle Sprain

Ligament sprains, chronic ankle instability, tendonitis, and recovery from ankle injuries.

Headache

Tension headaches, cervicogenic headaches, migraines related to neck dysfunction and muscle tension.

Sports Injuries

Athletic injuries, muscle strains, ligament tears, overuse injuries, and return-to-sport rehabilitation.

Plantar Fasciitis

Heel pain, foot arch pain, morning foot stiffness, and chronic plantar fascia inflammation.

Hip Pain

Hip arthritis, bursitis, labral tears, IT band syndrome, and post-hip replacement rehabilitation.

Osteoarthritis

Joint degeneration, cartilage wear, chronic joint pain, and mobility improvement for arthritis patients.

Frozen Shoulder

Adhesive capsulitis, shoulder stiffness, limited range of motion, and progressive shoulder rehabilitation.

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation

Recovery after orthopedic surgeries, joint replacements, ligament repairs, and surgical procedures.

Post Brain Stroke

Stroke recovery, neurological rehabilitation, mobility restoration, and functional independence.

Parkinson's Disease

Movement disorder management, balance improvement, gait training, and symptom control.

Cerebral Palsy

Motor function improvement, spasticity management, mobility enhancement, and developmental support.

Acidity

Visceral manipulation for acid reflux, digestive issues, and gastrointestinal discomfort management.

GERD

Gastroesophageal reflux disease management, esophageal health, and digestive system optimization.

Constipation

Visceral manipulation for bowel regularity, digestive health, and gastrointestinal function improvement.

Pelvic Misalignment

Pelvic floor dysfunction, alignment correction, and musculoskeletal balance restoration.

Diabetes Management

Exercise therapy, circulation improvement, neuropathy management, and metabolic health support.

Blood Pressure Management

Cardiovascular health, stress reduction, circulation improvement, and lifestyle modification support.

Our Treatment Approach

Thorough Assessment

Personalized Treatment

Customized treatment plans designed specifically for your condition, lifestyle, and recovery goals.

Active Rehabilitation

Progress Tracking

Regular reassessment and adjustment of your treatment plan to ensure optimal recovery outcomes.
