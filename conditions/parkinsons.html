Understanding Parkinson's Disease

Parkinson's disease is a progressive neurological disorder that affects movement, balance, and coordination.

At PhysioPulse, we provide specialized physiotherapy programs designed to manage Parkinson's symptoms, improve mobility, and enhance quality of life through evidence-based interventions.

Our Parkinson's Management Approach

🚶

Gait Training

Specialized walking exercises to improve stride length, reduce freezing, and enhance mobility.

⚖️

Balance Improvement

Balance training exercises to reduce fall risk and improve postural stability.

💪

Strength Building

Targeted strengthening exercises to maintain muscle function and prevent weakness.

🔄

Range of Motion

Gentle stretching to maintain joint flexibility and prevent stiffness.
