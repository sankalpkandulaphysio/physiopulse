# Trust & Social Proof

## Purpose
Establish credibility early and reduce anxiety for first-time visitors.

## Trust Signals to Include
- Patient ratings (e.g., Google reviews)
- Years of clinical experience
- Number of patients treated (only if verifiable)
- Professional credentials and certifications

## Placement Rules
- Near primary CTAs
- Below hero section on homepage
- On service and location pages

## Content Guidelines
- Avoid exaggerated claims
- Prefer specific, concrete statements
- Use real names where possible; otherwise anonymize respectfully

## Visual Treatment
- Neutral or light background
- Icons or subtle separators
- Compact layout, not overwhelming
