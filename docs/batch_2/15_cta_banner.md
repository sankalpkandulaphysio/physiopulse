# Call-to-Action (CTA) Banner

## Purpose
Drive bookings and enquiries at key decision points.

## Placement
- End of major sections
- Bottom of pages
- After testimonials

## CTA Types
- Book an Appointment
- Speak to a Physiotherapist
- Request a Call Back

## Design Rules
- High contrast background
- Clear, single primary action
- Minimal text

## Behaviour
- Scrolls to form or opens WhatsApp
- No payment flow
