# Hero Section

## Purpose
Immediate trust-building and conversion.

## Structure
- Full-width background image
- Blue overlay for contrast
- H1 headline
- Supporting paragraph
- Primary CTA (Book Appointment)

## Content Rules
- One clear message
- Focus on outcomes (movement, pain relief)
- Avoid medical promises

## Visual Rules
- Background image with human subject
- Subject offset to right
- Text aligned left

## CTA Behaviour
- Scrolls to booking / enquiry form
