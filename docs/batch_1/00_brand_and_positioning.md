# Brand & Positioning

## Brand Name
PhysioPulse (working name)

## Core Promise
Helping people in Hyderabad live healthier, pain-free lives through evidence-based physiotherapy and movement care.

## Brand Personality
- Professional and clinical, but warm and human
- Trust-building, never salesy
- Confident, not exaggerated

## Tone of Voice
- Clear, supportive, reassuring
- Avoid medical jargon unless explained
- Speak directly to the patient

## Trust Principles
- No false claims
- No guarantees of cure
- Emphasis on assessment, personalised plans, and progress

## Target Audience
- Working professionals (25–55)
- Seniors requiring mobility support
- Sports & fitness enthusiasts
- Corporate employees (workplace health)

## Geographic Focus
Hyderabad, India
