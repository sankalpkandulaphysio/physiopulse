# Service Page Template

## Goal
Educate users and convert intent-driven visitors.

## Structure
1. Hero (Service-specific headline)
2. Service Overview
3. Conditions Treated
4. Treatment Approach
5. Benefits & Outcomes
6. Testimonials (Relevant)
7. CTA Banner

## Content Guidelines
- Explain the service in simple language
- Focus on assessment and personalised care
- Avoid absolute claims

## SEO
- One primary keyword per service
- Internal links to related conditions and blogs
