# Homepage Composition

## Goal
Maximise trust and lead conversion within the first visit.

## Section Order (Top to Bottom)
1. Hero Section
2. Trust & Social Proof
3. Services Overview Grid
4. Why Choose Us (Value Propositions)
5. Testimonials
6. CTA Banner
7. Footer

## Above-the-Fold Rules
- Clear H1 with outcome-focused messaging
- Primary CTA visible without scrolling
- Supporting trust indicator (rating or experience)

## Behaviour
- Smooth scroll to forms
- No popups on first load
