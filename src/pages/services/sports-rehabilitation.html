What is Sports Rehabilitation?

Sports rehabilitation is a specialized branch of physiotherapy focused on treating athletic injuries and helping athletes return to peak performance. Whether you're a professional athlete, weekend warrior, or fitness enthusiast, our sports rehab programs are designed to get you back to doing what you love safely and effectively.

Our approach combines advanced assessment techniques, evidence-based treatments, sport-specific training, and injury prevention strategies. We don't just heal your injury—we help you come back stronger, faster, and more resilient.

Sports Injuries We Treat

Comprehensive treatment for all types of athletic and exercise-related injuries.

Knee Injuries

ACL tears, MCL sprains, meniscus injuries, runner's knee, patellar tendonitis, and post-surgical rehab.

Shoulder Injuries

Rotator cuff tears, shoulder impingement, labral tears, dislocations, and throwing injuries.

Ankle & Foot

Ankle sprains, Achilles tendonitis, plantar fasciitis, stress fractures, and instability issues.

Muscle Strains

Hamstring strains, quad tears, calf injuries, groin pulls, and muscle contusions.

Overuse Injuries

Tennis elbow, golfer's elbow, shin splints, IT band syndrome, and repetitive strain injuries.

Back & Spine

Sports-related back pain, stress fractures, disc injuries, and core muscle strains.

Our Sports Rehab Process

A progressive, goal-oriented approach to get you back to peak performance.

1

Injury Assessment

Detailed evaluation of your injury including movement analysis, strength testing, and sport-specific functional assessment to understand the full extent of the problem.

2

Recovery Planning

Create a structured rehabilitation timeline with clear phases from initial healing through return-to-sport, tailored to your sport and performance goals.

3

Active Rehabilitation

Progressive exercise program including strength training, mobility work, sport-specific drills, and neuromuscular control to restore function and confidence.

4

Return to Sport

Gradual reintegration to sport with performance testing, monitoring load management, and injury prevention strategies to ensure safe, sustainable return.
