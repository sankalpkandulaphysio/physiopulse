Neuro Rehabilitation

Specialized therapy for neurological conditions including stroke recovery, Parkinson's disease, cerebral palsy, and other neurological disorders

Comprehensive Neuro Rehabilitation

Our neuro rehabilitation program is designed to help individuals with neurological conditions regain function, improve mobility, and enhance quality of life. We provide evidence-based interventions tailored to each patient's specific needs.

Conditions We Treat

Stroke Recovery

Comprehensive rehabilitation for post-stroke patients focusing on motor function, balance, and daily living activities

Parkinson's Disease

Specialized therapy to improve mobility, balance, and reduce symptoms progression

Cerebral Palsy

Individualized programs to enhance motor skills, coordination, and functional independence

Multiple Sclerosis

Symptom management and functional improvement strategies

Spinal Cord Injuries

Rehabilitation focusing on mobility, strength, and adaptive techniques

Traumatic Brain Injury

Comprehensive recovery programs for cognitive and physical rehabilitation

Our Treatment Approach

1

Comprehensive Assessment

Detailed evaluation of neurological function, mobility, and specific challenges

2

Individualized Treatment Plan

Customized rehabilitation program based on assessment findings and patient goals

3

Functional Training

Focus on improving daily living activities and functional independence

4

Progress Monitoring

Regular assessments to track improvement and adjust treatment as needed
