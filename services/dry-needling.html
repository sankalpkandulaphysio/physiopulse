What is Dry Needling?

Dry needling is an evidence-based treatment technique that uses thin filiform needles to target trigger points in muscles, providing relief from pain and improving muscle function.

Our certified dry needling practitioner, Dr. Sankalp, has received specialized training from the United Kingdom and combines this technique with other manual therapy approaches for comprehensive treatment.
